Baked Feta Pasta

Recipe:

  • Pasta
  • Feta Cheese
  • Grape Tomatoes
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Basil

  1. This week's recipe is an Instagram find which turned out to be pretty decent
  2. Add grape tomatoes, olive oil, salt and pepper to a baking dish.
  3. Then add a block of feta cheese in the middle of the container.
  4. Bake it at 400 Fahrenheit for 30 minutes.
  5. When it comes out, add some garlic and fresh basil, giving it all a good mix.
  6. Then add some of that cooked pasta on top of it and mix everything together.
  7. Serve Hot

Yet another recipe to make the same dish.
