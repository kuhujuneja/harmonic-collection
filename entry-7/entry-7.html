Preparing your mentality

Preparing your mentality is the first step towards starting to cook for the first time.

1.Figure out the real reason you’re not cooking

You may feel the reason you’re not cooking every day is that you either
feel that you don’t have time or you feel like you don’t know how to
cook. For me the real reason I didn't cook for the longest time wasn’t that I
didn’t have the time, it was that I wasn’t prepared to cook. Before you start
cooking, you have to first know what you’re going to cook. And then you
have to buy the ingredients that go into that recipe. These 2 steps take up so
much mental energy that one just tends to fall back to running away from cooking.
It’s time for you to address this issue now!

2.Address the Issue

I think for me it’s just about making a schedule for oneself so now I prepare
myself in advance, shop accordingly and trust me overcoming this whole fiasco
has been a very liberating feeling. So start by making a grocery list according to
the recipes which you’ll make soon enough!

3.Start small

Let’s take baby steps to get where we want to! If you currently don’t cook at all,
start off by cooking once per week. You can follow the recipes which I have
attached later on this website as and when you reach that stage. Over time when
you feel more comfortable, increase to twice a week, and so on. Don’t just pull
the trigger and go to booking every single day, that’s just a long shot
for a beginner. Consistency is the key, too much ambition might hinder that.

4.Grow slowly

Slowly increase the number of days per week that you’re cooking. I mean
remember why you landed on this website was to learn how to cook so you did
have the motivation to cook just not the confidence. Now is your time to conquer,
hehe maybe a little extreme of my feelings!

5.Start with food that you enjoy

I’d suggest that you start off by preparing one of the recipes that I have provided
or prepare dishes that you enjoy. This would be a good motivation for you to get
started because trust me your zeal and passion will only come out when you’re
interested and what better interest than serving your tastebuds?

6.Share this with others

If you know of other people who are suffering from the I am lazy to cook but I
really want to problem -share this website with them (and this is not just a
marketing thing I am doing here) When multiple people get involved in a task
you’re more encouraged to put in more time and energy towards your newly
undertaken goal.

I am aware that building a new habit is never easy. That said, by breaking
it down into a few, smaller, easier-to-achieve steps, I know you can
succeed. After all, if a hopeless case like me can do it, I’m sure you can
too. You’ve already shown interest by reaching this far into my website
which is much more motivation than most people have. So get started,
I bet you’ll even begin to enjoy it. Let’s go to the next step and create an
actual Grocery List!
