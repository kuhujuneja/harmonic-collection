Aglio E Olio

Ingredients:

Spaghetti

Garlic

Olive Oil

Salt and Pepper

Chilli

Parsley

Parmesan Cheese

Recipe:

  1. Aglio-E-Olio is a tasty and easy recipe.
  2. First boil the spaghetti in a pot of lightly salted water.
  3. Put garlic and olive oil in a pan on medium heat and roast
    the garlic until it's brown.
  4. Now add your boiled spaghetti into the pan with salt,
    pepper and chilli powder to taste.
  5. Mix it all together and add parsley and cheese to it. Stir until combined
  6. Serve the hot delicious pasta with the remaining cheese.
