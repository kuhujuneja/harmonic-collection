Egg Rice

Ingredients:

Eggs

Rice

Salt and Pepper

Chilli

Diced Onion

Diced Tomato

Recipe:

  1. Egg Rice is a comfort food for me. It is super easy to make.
  2. First, cook the rice you'd need.
  3. On the other hand, take eggs in proportion to the rice.
  4. Then break the eggs in a different container, mix it thoroughly
    and add chopped onion, tomato and any other vegetables of your choice.
  5. Add salt, pepper and chilli powder according to taste.
  6. Take a pan and add your cooked rice with this mixture in it.
  7. Thoroughly stir them together and let them cook till the eggs are fully cooked.
  8. The dish is ready, enjoy!
