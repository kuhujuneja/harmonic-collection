French Toast

Ingredients:

Bread

Eggs

Milk

Honey

Salt and Pepper

Chilli

Butter

Recipe:

  1. French Toast is an easy breakfast recipe which I love
  2. Whisk milk, eggs, salt, pepper and chilli powder together in a bowl.
  3. Lightly butter a pan and heat over medium-high heat.
  4. Dunk bread in the egg mixture, soaking both sides.
  5. Transfer to the hot pan and cook until golden, 3 to 4 minutes per side
  6. Add honey on top of it and serve.
I don't think there are many complicated ways to make French Toast.
