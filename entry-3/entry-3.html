Chocolate Mousse

Ingredients:

Chocolate bar

Cocoa Powder

Butter

Powdered Sugar

Whipping Cream

Vanila Extract

Instant Coffee

Recipe:

  1. Chocolate Mousse is an easy to make at home dessert
  2. First, microwave butter, instant coffee and chopped chocolate bar
    together at 30-second intervals while stirring it until it's fully melted.
  3. Create another mixture by mixing the powdered sugar and cocoa powder
    together.
  4. Whip the heavy cream until frothy.
  5. Slowly add the powdered sugar-cocoa powder mixture a little at a time
    until fully combined.
  6. Then stir in the vanilla. Combine whipped cream with melted chocolate.
  7. Add some of the whipped cream to the melted chocolate bowl
    and fold it in until no streaks are left.
  8. Pipe with a bit of fresh whipped cream on top and sprinkle
    with some chocolate shavings.
